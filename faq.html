Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Formula Brewing

Why don't you list IBUs?

IBU is subjective and misleading. We recommend asking one of our beer tenders to point out which beer on tap at that time suits your preference for a beer with low, medium, or high bitterness from hops.

Do you have other drink options aside from beer?

We offer a variety of beverages aside from beer. We have lemonade, iced tea, cocktails, beer, apple juice, wine, hard kombucha, ginger beer, NA beer, and gluten free beer.

Do you sell kegs?

No. We do have growlers and crowlers for sale if you'd like to take home one of our beers.

Do you do flights?

No. If you're deciding between a couple beers, we're more than happy to provide you with a taste before deciding which one to purchase.

Do you have gluten free crust?

No, we currently don't carry gluten free crust. Ask your server for our gluten free options.

Do you have an allergens list?

Yes. Ask your server for our list of allergens.

Do you have gluten free options?

Yes. Ask your server for our list of options.

Do you have vegan options?

Yes. Ask your server for our list of options.

Do you have fries?

No. We do not have a fryer.

Can you bring your own food?

Outside food is not allowed. If someone in your party has a dietary restriction we can't accommodate, then approval will need to be given by the owner.

Can you bring dessert?

Cakes, cupcakes, pies, and cookies are allowed for a $20 dessert charge.

üìÖ Reservations & Events

Do you take reservations?

Yes. To submit a reservation request, and to see our large group policies, please fill out the reservation questionnaire found on our website at www.formulabrewing.com.

What is the cost for reservations?

We don't charge a reservation fee, cancellation fee, or have a spend minimum. Per our reservation policy, a 20% auto gratuity charge will be applied to all tabs associated with your event.

Do you rent out your entire brewery?

No. Our taproom and beer garden are open to everyone, but we do use reserved signs. If your party is a large party, we can use stanchions to rope off your section.

Can you decorate the area?

Decorations are allowed as long as it doesn't interfere with the experience of our other patrons and can easily be cleaned up.

Do you allow kids?

Yes. Formula Brewing is an all-ages establishment.

Do you allow dogs?

Yes. Well behaved dogs are allowed in the beer garden. Service dogs are allowed in the taproom.

Do you do table service?

No. We do counter service where you'll place your food and drink orders at the counter, and we'll deliver the food to your table.

What are your holiday hours?

Hours vary depending on the holiday and will be posted to our Facebook and Instagram pages.

Still have questions?

Our friendly staff is here to help! Visit us or give us a call.
